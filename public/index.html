How We Met

It was a crisp autumn morning at the local farmer's market when our paths first crossed. Sarah was examining fresh tomatoes while James was trying to decide between different types of artisanal bread. A chance collision over a basket of apples led to shared laughter, and the rest, as they say, is history.

Over countless coffee dates, weekend adventures, and shared dreams, our love grew stronger. Now, we're ready to begin our greatest adventure together, and we want you to be part of our special day.
